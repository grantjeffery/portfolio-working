Nasa TV Broadcast Package

Motion Graphics, Compositing, Audio Mixing

A short film project made possible by the generosity of friends, Full Tilt gave me the opportunity to try my hand at a pile of zany visuals, as well as the chance to spend a weekend building styrofoam mountains and rendering cheesy effects work.

CREDITS

Using newly released archival images and audio from the hayday of the Apollo program, I constructed this broadcast package as a love letter to the messy, heady early days of american space travel.
