Dau, The Devil and Ilya Khrzhanovsky

PROJECT DETAILS

Motion Graphics, Illustration, 3d

Dau is an ongoing, self directed motion project about the making of DAU, a Ukrainan film project that went dangerously off the rails. I used 3d and 2d motion graphics to illustrate the story of film director, become unlikely despot: Ilya Khrzhanovsky.
